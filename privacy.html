Privacy, in plain English.

Last updated: July 2026

This site has no forms, no analytics, no cookies and no tracking. The only way information reaches SevenSeers Consulting is when you choose to send an email.

One image on the site is loaded from this website's own server. Nothing is loaded from third‑party advertising or tracking networks.

Enquiries from employers

If you write about a mandate, the correspondence is kept for the duration of the engagement and as long afterwards as is reasonably needed for records and follow‑up work. It is not shared outside the mandate, and it is never sold or used for marketing.

CVs and candidate information

CVs received — whether sent directly or through a search — are held privately by Phaniraj Bhatt. A CV is shared with a client only for the specific mandate it relates to, and only with the candidate's knowledge. Assessment reports are shared with the commissioning client and, where agreed, with the candidate.

If you register in confidence

If you send a CV as a senior candidate rather than about a specific role, it is held privately and read only by Phaniraj Bhatt. You will not be put forward for any mandate without your explicit agreement first, and your details are never circulated to build a client's interest. If you would rather be forgotten after a conversation, say so and it is done.

Retention

CVs and candidate notes are retained so that candidates can be considered for future relevant roles. If a CV is clearly out of scope, it is deleted.

Removal

If you would like your CV, correspondence or details deleted, email phaniraj@sevenseers.com with "Remove my details" in the subject line. Removal is confirmed by reply, normally within a week.

Questions

Anything unclear, ask directly: phaniraj@sevenseers.com.
